Book Recs

What book suits YOU?, September 13, 2024

Choosing what kind of book to read can be hard, especially for first time readers. They don't know where to start. How about genre? What kind of genre's do you like? But you wouldn't know that yet. I'd say start experimenting, but choosing the wrong book can do more harm than good. Ask a close friend or relative abou what genre they think would suit you, or pick books with similar plots from shows or movies you like.

Mystery Series

So you've joined the dark side, have you?, September 14, 2024

Well, well, well. Are you a newbie who just happened to stumble upon a thrilling moving about Sherlock Holmes? Or perhaps Enola, his sister? Well, the Sherlock Holmes series, is a bummer. DO NOT READ IT. Its boring, and frankly, worse than Wonderland in terms of reality. For Beginners, I'd suggest more subtle school-themed books, like 'One of Us is Lying' by Karen M McManus, or even 'The House with a Hundred Clocks in the Walls'. Both are great for beginners and feel more like something you wouldn't be FORCED to read.
